Cupcakes are small, individual cakes, typically baked in muffin tins or paper cups, and often decorated with frosting or other toppings.
Here's a quick overview What they are: Small, individual cakes baked in cups or muffin tins. Ingredients: Similar to standard cakes, including flour, sugar, eggs, and butter. Flavor: Cupcakes can be flavored with various ingredients, like chocolate, vanilla, or fruit. Baking: They bake faster than larger cakes due to their small size. Decorations: Often topped with frosting, sprinkles, or other decorations.
History: The concept of cupcakes can be traced back to the 18th century, where they were originally referred to as "number cakes" or "1-2-3-4 cakes". Popularity: Cupcakes are a popular dessert for all occasions and festivities.
